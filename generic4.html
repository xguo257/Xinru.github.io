This project builds a database of mammal ranges and physical geographic environment in California to support the analysis of the impact of environment on mammal distribution.

The database that I build contains all of the mammal ranges data and environmental data of California, as well as the clear answers to all the research questions, including the generation of essential maps. The database should have potential to support other analyses. Results will eventually lead to a summary of mammal distribution pattern in California.
